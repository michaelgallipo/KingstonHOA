HOA Rules and Policies

General Rules

  • Speed Limit - 10mph. Traffic is one way.
  • Dogs are allowed but must be on a leash not to exceed six feet and are subject to Phoenix Barking or Howling Dog Policy.
  • BoD will conduct periodic inspections of visible home maintenance and front yards.
  • All landscape changes must be approved by the BoD

See Rules and Policy Document Below for complete HOA rules.

Vehicle Policy

  • Vehicles are to be parked under carport roofs.
  • Up to two additional vehicles may be parked in driveway if long enough.
  • Storing unused vehicles, trailers, RVs, etc is prohibited.
